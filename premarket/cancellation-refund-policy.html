Cancellation & Refund Policy

At Fincomerley, we provide non-tangible, irrevocable goods ("Digital Products") as part of our subscription services. Please note that cancellations are not permitted for digital products, as these goods are delivered immediately upon confirmation of the order and cannot be reversed

Subscription cancellations will only take effect at the end of the current billing cycle. Subscribers will continue to have full access to our services until the conclusion of their current subscription period (monthly, quarterly).

Refund Policy

As this is a digital product, refunds are not available. eBooks are non-refundable. However, if you’d like to try our pre-market report, we’re happy to share the next day’s report as a trial. Just contact us via WhatsApp or email.

Exceptional Circumstances:

In rare cases, such as errors or special circumstances, you may contact us to discuss a potential refund. These requests will be evaluated individually and granted only under exceptional conditions.

Billing Errors:

If you notice a billing error, such as an overcharge or duplicate charge, please notify us immediately. Upon confirmation of the error, we will issue a full refund for the incorrect amount. For all refund-related queries, please contact our support team for assistance.

Modifications to the Policy:

We reserve the right to update or modify this policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and it is your responsibility to review the policy periodically. If you have any questions or require further assistance, don’t hesitate to contact our support team.
